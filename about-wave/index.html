About Wave

Wave Groups > About Wave

 

Wave Engineering, Inc. is a Structural and Civil Engineering consulting firm. Our main area of expertise is in the field of Telecommunications, Foundation Inspections, Residential and Commercial buildings. We look beyond our client present day need to provide cost effective engineering design solution that offers future flexibility and longevity. The relationship with our repeat clients is a testimony of our quality of service and we remain committed to expanding our services to meet the ever-growing needs of our clients. We are dedicated to quality and client service and our plan is to continue to grow as time passes on.

Wave Realty, Inc. is a privately owned professional real estate brokerage, licensed and governed by the Texas Real Estate Commission. We have represented buyers, sellers, tenants, landlords and commercial investors throughout the DFW metroplex.
